Jax Disc Golf Weekend

Jacksonville, FL · The Sarge · 4 Courses · 3 Days
Friday – Sunday · Summerville → Jacksonville → Summerville
Course Overview

The Sarge

Hardeeville, SC

Wooded + water hazards. Concrete pads. 18 holes. Free. ~2 hrs from Summerville (on the way).

New World DGC

Cecil Field, Jax

38 holes, 4 layout configs. DGPT Championship history. Free. No pro shop — bring water & discs. ~15 min from Argyle Forest.

Fore Palms DGC

Ed Austin Park, Jax

18+6 bonus holes. 3 tee pads per hole. Multiple pin positions. Free. ~30 min east from Argyle Forest.

The Grove

Orange Park, FL

18 holes, country club feel. Turf pads, manicured fairways. $10/person tee time at GroveDiscGolf.com. ~10 min from Argyle Forest.
Heads up: New World no longer has a pro shop, restaurant, or trash cans. Pack water, snacks, and a trash bag. The Grove requires advance tee times booked online — do this now. Fore Palms can get crowded on weekends — early arrival is key.

Saturday Double Round Day
```
7:00 AM
COFFEE
7 Brew Coffee
7788 Normandy Blvd, Jacksonville
Drive-thru, open at 5:30 AM. Fast, solid, and right on the way to New World from Argyle Forest. Good if you want to grab and go.
7:30 AM
DISC GOLF
New World DGC — Red Course (18)
13715 Lake Newman St, Jacksonville
Play the championship Red layout first — this is the DGPT course. Links-style, open, windy, water in play. Rated Hard/Very Hard. No pro shop, no water stations. Bring everything you need. Budget 2–2.5 hours.
Pro tip: If the crew still has legs after Red, play Blue/White for another 18. That's the beauty of the Infinity Loop design — 4 different 18-hole combos from the same starting area.
~11:00 AM
LUNCH
Taqueria Cinco
809 Lomax St, Jacksonville (5 Points area)
Best tacos in Jax. Al pastor on weekends is a must. Fresh corn tortillas, actually spicy sauces, killer guac. Austin-quality in a Jax neighborhood. Back patio is great. ~20 min from New World.
~1:00 PM
DISC GOLF
Fore Palms DGC
11751 McCormick Rd, Jacksonville
One of the top 5 courses in Florida per reviewers. 18 holes + 6 bonus. Trees, open, woods, pond. 3 tee pads and rotating pin positions keep it fresh. Bag hooks on the course. Gets busy weekends — post-lunch timing should thin the crowd. ~25 min from 5 Points. Budget 2+ hours.
~4:00 PM
OPTIONAL
Hanna Park / Beach
500 Wonderwood Dr, Jacksonville
Only ~10 min from Fore Palms. $5 entry. Beach, lake, hiking/MTB trails. Good way to cool down after 36+ holes if the crew wants to decompress before dinner.
~6:00 PM
BREWERY
Aardwolf Brewing Company
1461 Hendricks Ave, Jacksonville (San Marco)
Long-standing local favorite. Great IPAs and stouts. Indoor/outdoor seating with a good vibe. Food trucks often out back. ~20 min from Hanna Park, right in the San Marco neighborhood.
~7:30 PM
DINNER
The Butt Hutt Smokehouse
3210 Roosevelt Blvd, Jacksonville
Hidden gem BBQ. Burnt ends, pulled pork, Florida fries, collards. Happy hour every day with extra specials by day of week. Great sauces. Worth the trip from San Marco. Closer to your home base. ~15 min from Aardwolf.
```

```
The Grove
2525 Country Club Blvd, Orange Park
Country club disc golf. Manicured fairways, turf tee pads, water features. 2 tees and 2 baskets on every hole = 4 layout options. Gated community — you need to book a tee time at GroveDiscGolf.com ($10/person). Restrooms and water on course. Clubhouse has restaurants, simulators, pickleball if anyone wants more. ~10 min from Argyle Forest. Budget 2 hours.
Book The Grove tee times now. It's a gated community. No reservation = no entry. Go to GroveDiscGolf.com and book your slots for Sunday morning.
```
